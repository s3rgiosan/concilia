Welcome to Concilia

Concilia reconciles your bank statements with receipts using AI. Before we start, we need two things:

  • A folder where your receipts are organised by year/month
  • A Google Cloud service account key for Gemini AI

This wizard takes about a minute. You can change everything later in Settings.

Choose your receipts folder

Pick the root folder. Inside, Concilia expects subfolders like 2025/01/receipts/ containing your receipt PDFs and images.

Gemini service account key

Concilia uses Google Gemini (Vertex AI) to read receipts. Provide a service account JSON key with access to Vertex AI in your Google Cloud project.
